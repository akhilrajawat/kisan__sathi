👨‍🌾 About Kisan Sathi

Kisan Sathi is a simple yet powerful platform built to connect farmers directly with buyers across India. Whether you're growing crops or looking to purchase them, Kisan Sathi makes it easier, faster, and more transparent — all without middlemen.

🌱 Why Kisan Sathi?

  • ✅ Farmers can list crops with weight, variety, and price.
  • ✅ Buyers can explore listings and contact sellers directly.
  • ✅ No commissions or hidden charges — we promote fair trade.

🤝 Who Can Use It?

  • 👩‍🌾 Farmers looking to sell their crops directly.
  • 🧑‍💼 Buyers interested in sourcing fresh produce from local farms.
  • 🌍 Anyone promoting rural upliftment and transparency in agriculture.

🔐 Trust & Simplicity

We focus on making things simple and reliable. No online payments are handled through our app — all transactions happen personally between buyer and seller. Our job is just to connect you both.

🚀 Our Vision

To empower every farmer in India by giving them easy access to digital tools, broader markets, and better profits. Together, let’s make agriculture smarter and more sustainable.

Let’s grow together. 🌾
